About Me

I am current a PhD student at the HCP lab in the Sun Yat-sen University, advised by Professors Guanbin Li and Liang Lin. I hold the Bachelor degree in Information and Computing Science in 2017 from South China Normal University. After that, I earned my Master degree in Computer Technology in 2020 from Xiamen University, under the guidance of Professor Cheng Wang in ASC lab.

My research interests are centered around computer vision, specifically in image and video generation. Previously, I have explored point cloud processing. My goal is to create virtual worlds.
